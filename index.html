Long Nguyen Tan

I am a final-year Computer Science student at the National University of Singapore, specializing in Natural Language Processing and Artificial Intelligence. My academic journey is generously supported by a full-ride scholarship and various grants from NUS. My research focuses on advancing NLP and AI methodologies to build socially aware, ethically grounded models that enhance diagnostics, enable personalized interventions, and ensure fairness in high-stakes decision-making environments.

Previously, I was at ImpriMed, Raffles Medical Group, Sustainable and Green Finance Institute, and Energy Studies Institute.

I am deeply thankful to the mentors who have guided me along the way, generously sharing their expertise and insights. If you are interested in exploring NLP research and would like to connect, please don’t hesitate to reach out.
